Hello, I'm Mohammad H. Erfani

Mohammad H. Erfani is a postdoctoral research scientist at NASA's Goddard Institute for Space Studies (GISS) and the Center for Climate Systems Research (CCSR) at Columbia University. His research focuses on integrating Machine Learning into hydrologic and Earth system models. Before joining CCSR, he was involved in research on flood detection and early warning systems using Computer Vision and Machine Learning. Mohammad holds a Ph.D. in Civil and Environmental Engineering with a specialization in Water Resources Management from the University of South Carolina.
